Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 April 2025

This policy explains how Will Fazackerley ("I", "me") collects and uses personal data when you submit feedback or sign up to receive updates via RefBoard (the "app"). I am the data controller for the purposes of UK GDPR and EU GDPR.

1. What data I collect

When you submit the feedback form in the app, you may provide:

  • Your email address (optional — only if you choose to provide it for updates)
  • Your feedback text and any other responses you enter in the form

I do not use cookies, analytics, or any other tracking on this app. The app itself stores no personal data.

2. Why I collect it and the legal basis

  • Email address: To send you occasional product updates about RefBoard. Legal basis: your consent (Article 6(1)(a) UK/EU GDPR). You can withdraw consent at any time by unsubscribing or emailing me.
  • Feedback text: To understand how to improve the app. Legal basis: my legitimate interest in developing a better product (Article 6(1)(f) UK/EU GDPR).

3. Who I share it with

Your data is processed by Tally (tally.so), the form provider used to collect responses. Tally acts as a data processor on my behalf. Tally is headquartered in Belgium and processes data within the EU. You can read Tally's privacy policy at tally.so/help/privacy-policy.

I do not sell, rent, or share your data with any other third parties.

4. How long I keep it

  • Email address: Until you unsubscribe or ask me to delete it.
  • Feedback text: For as long as it is useful for product development, and no longer than 2 years from submission.

5. Your rights

Under UK and EU GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data I hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability (for data processed by consent)
  • Withdraw consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, email me at refboardprivacy@gmail.com. I will respond within 30 days.

6. Complaints

If you are in the UK, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO). If you are in the EU, you may contact your local supervisory authority.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact: refboardprivacy@gmail.com
